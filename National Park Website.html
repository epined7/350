About Yosemite
Welcome to Yosemite National Park. Here you will explore the 1187 square mile landscapes filled with granite mountains, expansive forests, and beautiful waterfalls. Hike through and visit the spectacular sights Yosemite has to offer. Or maybe take a bike, or even a tour bus. Reserve a camp spot, and stargaze at night. Bring all the right gear and stay safe, we hope you will enjoy your visit!
BIKING
Enjoy a nice bike ride through our 12 miles of bike paths. Or bike on our regular roadways.
HIKING
Hike through the beautful trails, and see the vistas, granite mountains, and waterfalls.
CAMPING
13 popular campgrounds. Reserve quick, first come first serve.
TOUR GUIDES
Yosemite Hospitality offers bus tours year round. Get your tickets and see sights like the giant sequoias, Yosemite Valley, Glacier Point, and many more.
STARGAZING
Gaze at the stars in the peaceful nights at Yosemite.
