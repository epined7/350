My 10
Design Principles
1
Good design has space
There should be room for the eye to rest in most cases.
2
Good design should be creative
There should be interesting elements if possible. Things that differ from what is commonly seen in the world today. Don’t feel too pressured to do what everyone else is doing.
3
Good design should be consistent
There should be a common design language, or a common feel to the design. Too many conflicting styles creates visual confusion.
4
Good design isn’t over detailed
Try not to have too many things, sometimes it’s better to remove as much as possible. Search to remove unnecessary items in a composition. This also depends on the context of what the work is meant for.
5
Good design should be long lasting
The design should try and hold up for years to come. Shouldn’t be outdated soon.
6
Good design should be fully cared for
Don’t be lazy about some things, take the time to think it through and go through many design concepts.
7
Good design is understandable
The viewer should be able to understand what he/she is looking at. It shouldn’t be a stressful experience.
8
Good design has a good use of color or value
The colors should elicit the reaction you were looking for.
9
Good design be expressive
There should be a feeling of the designer’s own personality in the design.
10
Good design should have a sense of balance
The design should have balanced elements. Nothing should look out of place.
